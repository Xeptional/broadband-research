About Us

Welcome to the University of Ibadan Research Project! Our mission is to spearhead a revolutionary transformation in Nigeria's broadband infrastructure. While our vision encompasses the entire country, we'll be commencing our groundbreaking work in five carefully selected pilot states: Kogi, Oyo, Imo, Cross River, and Kano. Our commitment is to ensure inclusive and reliable internet access for all. Explore our methodology, track our progress, and witness the transformative impact of our efforts.
